About Ecosun Solar Power Limited

Ecosun Solar Power Limited — the maker of Ecosun Power Products — is a premier provider of innovative solar energy solutions. We are committed to delivering sustainable, clean energy through cutting-edge technologies. Partnering with top European and Asian companies, Ecosun has been at the forefront of solar technology innovation since its founding. Our products include high-efficiency solar panels, advanced solar inverters, state-of-the-art lithium batteries, and AI-powered solar streetlights.

Through our evolving technology and partnerships, Ecosun has expanded its expertise in providing reliable energy solutions for government and corporate projects worldwide. Our systems are currently deployed across countries such as Peru, South Africa, Ghana, Ethiopia, Zimbabwe, Pakistan, Vietnam, and the Philippines — driving the global shift towards renewable energy.

In Nigeria, our solutions have powered communities and organizations in over six states, including FCT-Abuja, Delta, Lagos, and Rivers State. Whether for residential, commercial, or government projects, Ecosun delivers sustainable energy that empowers lives and drives growth.

Our solar solutions are categorized into two key areas. First, we provide premium solar panels, solar inverters, and lithium batteries known for superior performance and durability. Our panels offer module efficiencies of up to 21.33%, ensuring maximum energy yield even in low-light conditions. Our pure sine wave solar inverters feature smart technologies like WiFi and GPRS compatibility, high PV input voltage range, smart battery charge designs, and seamless off-grid and on-grid capabilities.

Second, we are pioneers in integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the Internet of Things (IoT) into solar-powered CCTV streetlights. These revolutionary systems feature voice and facial recognition, GPS tracking, remote monitoring, anti-theft systems, and intelligent management, providing enhanced urban security solutions.

Our key inverter features include:

  • WiFi and GPRS compatibility for iOS and Android apps
  • Ability to operate without a battery
  • One-key restoration to factory settings
  • Built-in lithium battery automatic activation
  • Integrated MPPT solar chargers (160A for 8kW/10kW models)
  • High PV input voltage range (90 - 500 VDC)
  • Built-in anti-dust kit for harsh environments
  • Smart battery charge design to extend battery life
  • Dual output with touch button controls
  • On-grid and off-grid operation modes

Our Solar Home Systems are specially engineered to meet the demanding needs of healthcare facilities. They provide uninterrupted power for critical medical equipment, lighting, and refrigeration. Featuring advanced lithium-ion battery storage, these systems ensure continuous energy supply even without sunlight and offer scalability to accommodate growing power demands.

By offering reliable, sustainable, and easy-to-maintain energy solutions, Ecosun Solar Power supports the crucial operations of healthcare centers where power stability is vital.

Our solar modules achieve up to 21.33% efficiency, ensuring maximum power output and minimal kilowatt-hour costs over their lifetime. Designed with anti-crack and Multi-Busbar (MBB) technologies, our panels maintain excellent performance even under harsh conditions like low-light, snow loads (up to 500Pa), and wind loads (up to 2400Pa).

With superior resistance to potential-induced degradation (PID) and minimal annual power attenuation, our modules offer lasting reliability and higher returns on investment, making them ideal for diverse solar applications.
